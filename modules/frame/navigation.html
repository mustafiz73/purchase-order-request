How to submit a purchase order request? Click 'Purchase Order Request', click 'New', fill information and save. Click 'Items' for adding items. Click Files for uploading supporting files. Finnaly click 'Alert' for sending an alert email to your manager for approving.

How to approve a purchase order request? Click 'Purchase Order Request', find the request, click 'Approving', tick the check box and save. Finnaly click 'Alert' for sending an alert email to an accountant for processing.

How to process a purchase order request? Click 'Purchase Order Request', find the request, click 'Processing', tick the check box and save. Finnaly click 'Alert' for sending an alert email to the requester.
